// Doing Laundry //
TESTING
1. Gather Dirty Clothes
  1.1 Collect All Clothes That One Wishes to Washing
    1.1.1 place all dirty clothes into a single vessel
    1.1.2 physically move vessel of dirty clothes next to washing machine

2. Locate Washing Machine
  2.1 Open Lid to Washing Machine
  2.2 Move Dirty Clothes from the vessel they are currently in to inside the drum of the washing Machine
  2.3 Pour Contents of Measuring Cup on top of Clothes in Washing Machine drum
    2.3.1 pick up measuring cup
  2.4 Close Lid of Washing machine
    2.4.1 use your hand to grab hold of the washing machine lid and lower it
  2.5 Start Washing Cycle
    2.5.1 turn dial of washing machine to desired wash mode
      2.5.1.1 use your hand to physically turn dial on washing machine
        2.5.1.1.1 power on washing machine
          2.5.1.1.1.1 use your hand to turn on power button of washing machine


3. Locate Washing Machine Detergent
  3.1 Open Cap to Container of Detergent
  3.2 Locate Measuring Cup for Detergent
  3.3 Pour Desired amount of Contents of Detergent Container into Measuring Cup
    3.3.1 pick up detergent container with your hands
